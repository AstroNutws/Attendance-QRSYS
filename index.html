Frequently Asked Questions
FAQ

Questions & Answers

What is the difference between Admin types? ▼
Standard Admins handle daily operations like monitoring attendance and managing students. Super Admins are system overseers with the exclusive authority to create new admin accounts, update credentials, and access the Super Admin management panel.
What can the Super Admin do that Standard Admins cannot? ▼
Super Admins can register new administrator accounts, modify peer details such as email addresses and usernames, reset passwords, and delegate Super Admin status to other accounts — all through the dedicated Super Admin Panel.
Who has direct access to the database? ▼
Neither Standard nor Super Admins have direct database access. Database ownership is strictly isolated to the Client (your organization) via their private Supabase Dashboard. System management and data storage are fully separated — regardless of whether your org is a school, company, or corporate group.
How are passwords and credentials protected? ▼
Passwords are visually masked in the application UI to prevent shoulder-surfing. Future versions will introduce server-side cryptographic hashing for complete backend protection of all stored credentials.
