01Features

Everything You Need to Track Attendance

One platform, two clients, built for any size organization — from a classroom of 30 to a company of thousands.

📷
Webcam QR Scanning
Desktop app uses your existing webcam to scan QR codes in real time. No extra hardware needed — point, scan, done.
☁️
Instant Cloud Sync
Every scan hits your Supabase PostgreSQL database in real time. View records the moment they're logged — from anywhere.

4-Tier RBAC
Normal User → Admin → Super Admin → Owner. Every role is scoped precisely — no privilege leakage, ever.

Android Companion App
Lightweight .NET MAUI mobile client for on-the-go attendance management. Push notifications keep everyone in sync.

Full Audit Logs

You Own Your Data
QrSys is pure middleware. The dev team has zero access to your database — your Supabase keys, your data, your control.

08Frequently Asked Questions
FAQ

Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know before deploying QrSys for your organization.

What are the four roles in Project QrSys?▾
Normal User — has their own QR code and views their own attendance records. Admin — handles daily operations: scans for users, manages sections, approves requests. Super Admin — creates Admin accounts, manages credentials, and delegates Super Admin status. Owner — holds the database with full system authority via their private Supabase Dashboard.
What exactly can a Normal User do?▾
Normal Users can view their QR code for Admins to scan — marking their attendance — and view their own records. They can also submit a request to update personal information, but this requires Admin approval before it takes effect.
Can Admins scan QR codes on behalf of a user?▾
Yes. Admins can scan QR codes on behalf of users — useful when a user cannot scan themselves. Admins also manage sections, approve info change requests, and have full Admin Panel access.
What is the difference between Admin and Super Admin?▾
Admins handle day-to-day operations. Super Admins go higher: they can create new Admin accounts, modify peer credentials, reset passwords, and delegate Super Admin status via the dedicated Super Admin Panel.
Who has direct access to the database?▾
Only the Owner has database-level authority — exclusively through their private Supabase Dashboard. Normal Users, Admins, and Super Admins have zero direct database access. The QrSys development team also has zero access to your database at any time.
Do I need to manage my own server or cloud infrastructure?▾
No. QrSys uses Supabase as the backend, which handles all hosting for you. You simply own the Supabase project — we set it up, you keep the keys. No DevOps experience required.
Is there a cost to use QrSys?▾
The client applications fee are discussed with the developer. Contact us to get your organization account configured — we'll walk you through the setup with Supabase, or other postgresql that has pooler connections!
